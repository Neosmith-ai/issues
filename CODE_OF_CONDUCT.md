# Code of Conduct

## TL;DR

Be kind. Be specific. Don't post credentials. Don't punch down.

## Pledge

We want this space to be useful to engineers at every level — from someone
trying NeoSmith for the first time to a seasoned SRE debugging a production
outage. In service of that, we expect everyone participating here to:

- **Be respectful.** Assume good faith. Answers you find obvious may not be.
- **Be specific.** "Doesn't work" is less helpful than "returns 500 on
  requests with `max_tokens > 4096`."
- **Respect privacy.** Don't post API keys, confidential prompts, or anyone
  else's customer data — yours or theirs. Rotate any key you accidentally expose.
- **Use the right channel.** P1 issues go to your private chat. Security goes
  to `security@neosmith.ai`. Public issues are for the rest.

## Unacceptable

- Harassment, discrimination, or attacks on any person or group
- Sharing another customer's data, internal domains, or inferred org structure
- Posting secrets (keys, tokens, private URLs)
- Spam, off-topic promotion
- Drive-by negativity without a concrete improvement suggestion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or delete content, close issues without response, or
block users who violate this policy. Severe or repeated violations escalate
to GitHub. For questions about enforcement, email `contact-us@neosmith.ai`.
